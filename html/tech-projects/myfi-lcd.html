The Project

The "MyFi" LCD is an LCD which you can control with your phone over your WLAN.
You can type any text (limited to 16 characters in every line) into the 2 lines of the LCD.
You also can choose the background light color of the LCD with 3 sliders: red, green and blue.
In the app is a color preview over the sliders.
The Arduino with the LCD has to be connected to the same network as your phone!
